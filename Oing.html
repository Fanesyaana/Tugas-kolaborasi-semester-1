Plastic Waste and Bottles

Plastic Waste and Bottles

Plastic waste is the most common type of waste found in our surroundings. Due to its non-biodegradable nature, plastic waste is a major cause of environmental pollution. However, as time progresses, many people have started utilizing plastic waste. One example is making ecobrick tables.

The main material for this table is ecobrick, which is a used plastic bottle filled with plastic waste until it is solid. In addition, cardboard is used for the top part. The first step is to collect used plastic bottles and plastic waste. The plastic is then cut into small pieces and inserted into the bottle until it is solid. The finished ecobricks are then attached to the cardboard.

The advantage of ecobrick tables is that the manufacturing process does not require machinery, making it more environmentally friendly. This table also has an attractive visual appearance. By making ecobrick tables, we can educate the public about the importance of sorting waste and utilizing it.

The utilization of plastic waste into ecobrick tables is a very beneficial innovation. With materials from used waste, we can create attractive and environmentally friendly furniture. So instead of being thrown away, it's better to utilize it into something useful and beneficial.
